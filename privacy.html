Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you use ScoreMyAgency, we store only anonymized ratio scores — numbers like your gross margin percentage, Days Sales Outstanding, cash runway, revenue concentration, and revenue per employee. These aggregate scores are used to power the peer benchmark comparisons shown in your report.

We do not store the raw financial figures you enter (revenue amounts, expense totals, cash balance, headcount), your agency name, or any identifying information. Once your report is generated, your input data is discarded. There is no way to trace a benchmark data point back to your agency.

What we don't store

We do not store: raw financial inputs, agency names, IP addresses, email addresses (unless you voluntarily join the waitlist), or payment information. We do not sell data to third parties or share it with advertisers.

Waitlist

If you choose to join the monthly update waitlist, we store your email address and the composite score from your session. This information is used only to send you score updates. You can request removal at any time by emailing hello@scoremyagency.com.

Cookies and tracking

We do not set tracking cookies or use advertising pixels. The tool runs on Streamlit Cloud, which may set basic session cookies required for the application to function. We have no access to individual-level analytics from Streamlit's infrastructure.

Third-party services

The scoring tool is hosted on Streamlit Community Cloud. AI-generated findings use the Anthropic API. Your financial inputs are not retained by either service beyond the active session.

Questions

Email hello@scoremyagency.com with any privacy questions or data removal requests.
