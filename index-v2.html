Common questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before you start.

Is it really free?+
Yes, completely. There's no freemium tier, no trial, no credit card. ScoreMyAgency is free for all digital agencies. The tool is built to help the community, not to upsell you into a subscription.
Is my financial data safe?+
Absolutely. Your data is processed in real-time only and is never stored on our servers. We have no database of agency financials. Once your session ends, nothing is retained.
Do I need to use Xero or QuickBooks?+
No. While we support direct CSV exports from Xero and QuickBooks Online for the smoothest experience, you can also enter your numbers manually. If you have a P&L in any format, you can use it.
How accurate are the benchmarks?+
Our benchmarks are sourced from publicly available industry research on digital agencies and aggregated from 50+ data points. They're calibrated for agency-type businesses, not generic SMEs.
What size agency is this built for?+
ScoreMyAgency works best for digital agencies with 2–100 employees. The benchmarks and ratios are calibrated for this range. Freelancers and large networks may find some metrics less relevant.
Can I share my report with my team?+
Yes. Every report includes a downloadable PDF you can share with your management team, investors, or accountant. It's formatted to be clear to non-financial readers too.
